FAQ

Simple answers to the questions that matter.

Is this a loan or financing product?+
Neither. The Momentum Program is a prepaid savings plan — no interest, no credit check, no debt. You save at your pace and credits pay for the work.
What if I can't keep up with payments?+
Just let us know. You can reduce your monthly amount anytime, or apply your saved credits to a smaller scope of work. Momentum doesn't mean rigid.
Can I cancel and get my money back?+
Yes. Cancel anytime and receive your credit balance minus a small program fee. Many clients prefer to apply credits to a smaller project instead.
What if prices change before I'm funded?+
We check in at milestones to keep your estimate current. Before any work begins, we do an onsite walk-through to finalize everything.
Can family members contribute to my plan?+
Yes. Anyone can call us and we'll post a payment to your account manually. A shareable link is coming soon.
