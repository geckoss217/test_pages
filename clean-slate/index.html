FAQ

Straight answers.

Is this a loan or financing?+
No. The Clean Slate Plan is a prepaid savings program — no interest, no credit check, no debt. You save monthly and credits pay for your project when funded.
Can I cancel?+
Yes, anytime. If you want a refund, we return your balance minus a small program fee. Many clients prefer to apply credits to a smaller scope of work rather than cancel.
What if prices change before my project?+
We check in at milestones to keep estimates current. Before any work starts, we do an onsite walk-through to finalize everything. No surprises.
Can I pay more some months?+
Absolutely. Just let us know and we'll update your plan. You'll hit your clean slate sooner.
Can family members contribute?+
Yes. Anyone can call us and we'll post a payment to your account manually. A shareable link is coming soon.
